A New Era in RevOps Frameworks – Beyond the Traditional Models

Contents
  1. Introduction
  2. The Limitations of Traditional Frameworks
  3. Introducing Lucrative: The Industry-First RevOps Framework

Introduction

We’ve always prided ourselves on being at the forefront of innovation in Revenue Operations (RevOps). Our monthly updates are more than just news, they are reflections of our commitment to pioneering change in the industry. Unlike many firms, we don’t simply adhere to established frameworks; instead, we continuously adapt and create new models that better serve the evolving needs of businesses.

The Limitations of Traditional Frameworks

Over the course of more than 500 implementations, we’ve seen firsthand how traditional frameworks like BANT, SPICE, or ABX have their merits. However, we’ve also observed that these frameworks, while useful in certain contexts, often fall short when it comes to addressing the complexities that businesses face today. For a long time, we used these frameworks across a variety of industries, but it became clear that a one-size-fits-all approach was not the answer.

Introducing Lucrative: The Industry-First RevOps Framework

Here’s where the breakthrough comes: Lucrative, the first industry-first framework in RevOps that has been designed to address the unique, multi-faceted nature of modern business structures. Unlike traditional frameworks that rely on a set of rigid rules or metrics, Lucrative is dynamic and adaptable, built around two core pillars: Core Engine and Modules.

The Core Engine is at the heart of this framework, founded on the RACI (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed) model. Why? Because RevOps is not about just one team or one process. Let’s take Marketing Qualified Leads (MQL) as an example: traditionally, MQLs are defined solely by the marketing team. But in Lucrative, MQLs are a cross-functional responsibility. Marketing may drive the process, but the Sales and Customer Success (CS) teams must also be involved for true implementation and impact.

Evolving with Modern Business Realities

This approach reflects the reality of today’s business environment, where success is not achieved in silos. As businesses evolve and restructure, so too must the Core Engine. That’s why we have developed multiple Core Engines that can be selected based on your company’s specific needs, business models, and structure.

Once you’ve chosen your Core Engine, the next step is selecting the right Module. Modules are tailored to your business operations. Whether you’re in a short sales cycle environment, a longer B2B or B2C cycle, or practicing ABM (Account-Based Marketing), the right module ensures that your RevOps framework is aligned with your strategic goals.

We’ve spent years refining this approach, and we believe it represents the most powerful system available to businesses today. Our Core Engine + Module framework is flexible, adaptable, and constantly evolving, so your business stays ahead of the curve. We are committed to continuous improvement, ensuring that this system won’t fail until its next release, as we build in real-time improvements based on client feedback and market changes.

Join the Revolution in RevOps

We invite you to explore this revolutionary approach to RevOps and share your thoughts with us. How do you see this framework impacting your business? We’d love to hear your feedback and discuss how it could benefit your company.

At Mountainise, we don’t just keep up with the industry; we lead it. Join us as we continue to shape the future of Revenue Operations.

Rethinking Technology Decisions: The Lucrative Framework for Sustainable Growth

Introduction

When a CEO of a fast-growing enterprise recently asked,

“What’s the ROI of our marketing this year?” The room went quiet.

Not because it was a complex question, but because no one could answer it confidently.

Despite impressive revenue and a modern tech stack, there was no single version of truth.

After working with 500+ companies across SaaS, finance, and publishing, we’ve seen this pattern repeatedly:

Most organizations don’t fail due to a lack of technology. They fail due to fragmented decisions.

At Mountainise, we built the Lucrative Unified RevOps Framework to solve that.
 It’s how we help companies simplify complexity, align systems, and engineer sustainable growth.

The Problem: Growth Without Governance

Every team has its tools. Marketing automates, Sales tracks, Customer Success measures — but leadership can’t answer basic questions like:

  • Where do our best customers come from?
  • What’s our true CAC payback period?
  • How consistent is our forecasting accuracy?

This isn’t a technology gap; it’s a governance gap. The average company runs 30+ SaaS tools, yet still lacks shared definitions and accountability. Without alignment, data becomes fragmented, and growth stalls.

The Core Insight: Alignment Is the New ROI

True ROI doesn’t come from automation, it comes from alignment. That’s the foundation of the Lucrative Core Engine, which powers every transformation we deliver.

  1. Governance & RACI: One operating rhythm across teams.
     Weekly RevOps stand-ups, monthly KPI reviews, and documented decision logs.
  2. Canonical Data & Taxonomy: One language for every object, stage, and metric.
     A shared data dictionary ensures marketing and sales finally see the same numbers.
  3. Routing, SLAs & Handoffs: From lead to renewal, nothing falls through the cracks.
     Clear ownership and automation rules maintain velocity and accountability.
  4. Analytics Spine: Real-time visibility across ARR, NRR, CAC, and LTV.
     Weekly forecast accuracy becomes a measurable, trackable discipline.

When these are unified, companies can scale any GTM motion. Velocity (Funnel Ops), Targeted Accounts (ABX), or Product-Led Growth (PLG) with confidence.

The Lucrative 6D Delivery Model

Our process transforms adoption from chaos into clarity through six predictable phases:

Phase

      Outcome

           Key Focus

Discover

       Identify gaps & friction

            Stakeholder interviews, data audit

Define

       Lock KPIs, Taxonomy, and SLAs

            Blueprint, Governance Cadence

Design

       Create Detailed Build Plan

             Field Mapping, Routing, Dashboards

Develop

        Configure & Integrate

            Workflows, Automations, Testing

Deploy

        Launch & Enable Teams

             UAT, Playbooks, Training

Drive

        Optimize Post-launch

            Weekly Ops, QBRs, Backlog Refinement

This model ensures technology aligns with both short-term wins and long-term scalability.

Patterns Across 500+ Implementations

Our data consistently reveals:

  • 60 % of ROI loss comes from decision latency, not software choice.
  • Adopting a canonical taxonomy improves data quality 3–5× within 90 days.
  • Teams with clear SLAs and handoff playbooks reduce cycle time by up to 35 %.
  • Companies using multi-touch attribution improve marketing ROI accuracy by 40 %.

Technology becomes a multiplier only when tied to governance, data, and accountability.

RevOps as a Living System

Most companies treat tech as a one-time project.
We treat it as a living system – designed to evolve.

That’s why the final “D” in our model, Drive, is critical:

  • 30-day hypercare to stabilize performance.
  • Weekly RevOps stand-ups for SLA tracking.
  • Quarterly KPI reviews to realign growth goals.

In fast-moving markets, adaptability is the real differentiator.

The New Mandate for Growth Leaders

If your organization is asking, “How can we make smarter technology decisions?” – the answer isn’t another platform.

 It’s a unified framework that connects people, process, platform, and performance.

That’s what Lucrative delivers – clarity, consistency, and measurable growth.

Our Thesis

The companies that win the next decade won’t be the ones with the most software but the ones with the most alignment. Technology should move with your business, not against it.

That’s what makes growth truly Lucrative.
